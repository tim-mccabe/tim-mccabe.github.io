Mapping out the Top 100 Golf Courses in the U.S.

Golf Digest 2019-2020 rankings broken down

Posted by Tim McCabe on May 18, 2020

Golf Digest provides tons of insights about what are the best courses in the United States and how they stad up to one another. But the website has never provided a map that shows where each one of these exactly is. I put together this map for people to be able to see everythin across the country and to hopefully plan a golf trip to a location where someone could play a few of these in one trip.

There is nothing worse then planning a golf trip months in advance only to find out there was another top 100 course 30 minutes away that you didnt know about. Hopefully people can use this map in all fo there trips going forward to get the most out of golfing adventures.
